## Event Highlights

- **Expert Keynotes** - Leading cybersecurity professionals sharing real-world insights
- **Interactive Workshops** - Hands-on sessions covering incident response procedures
- **Networking Opportunities** - Connect with peers and industry leaders
- **Compliance Deep-Dive** - Detailed guidance on NIS2 requirements
